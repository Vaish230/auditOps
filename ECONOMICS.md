# Economics

If Credex deployed this tool tomorrow, here's how the unit economics could look.

## Value of a converted lead to Credex

A "converted lead" means a startup that:

1. Completes the audit (free)
2. Books a consultation with Credex
3. Purchases discounted AI credits

Assume the average startup spends $2,000/month on AI tools (a reasonable blend: $20‑$100/seat across 10‑50 person teams). Credex sells credits at a 20‑30% discount, taking a 10‑15% margin. On $2,000/month spend, Credex earns $200‑$300/month in margin.

- **Annual margin per customer:** $2,400‑$3,600
- **Average customer lifetime (SaaS credits):** 18 months → $3,600‑$5,400 lifetime value (LTV)

So a converted lead is worth roughly **$3,000‑$5,000 in lifetime margin**.

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by channel

From the GTM plan:

- **Cold DMs (X/LinkedIn):** 50 users acquired with $0 ad spend, but ~20 hours of founder time. At a nominal $50/hour, CAC ≈ $20/user.
- **Community posts (Reddit/Slack):** 30 users acquired with $0. CAC ≈ $0, but unpredictable.
- **Referrals (existing users):** 20 users acquired at $0. CAC ≈ $0.
- **Blended CAC near $0** in the first month because all channels are manual/earned.

If Credex later runs paid ads (e.g., LinkedIn ads at $5‑10 CPC, 2% conversion to audit), CAC rises to $250‑$500 per audit, still far below LTV.

## Conversion funnel estimates

| Stage                           | Conversion rate | Rationale                                                                                                        |
| ------------------------------- | --------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Audit completed → lead captured | 60%             | Lead capture is optional and post‑value. 60% seems plausible based on similar free‑tool gated‑report benchmarks. |
| Lead → consultation booked      | 10‑20%          | Only high‑savings audits ($500+/month) will be proactively contacted. Credex may convert 1‑2 out of 10 leads.    |
| Consultation → credit purchase  | 20‑30%          | Warm leads shown real savings; credit purchase is a no‑brainer for some.                                         |
| **Overall: audit → customer**   | **1.2‑3.6%**    | (60% × 15% × 25% = 2.25% midpoint)                                                                               |

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 months

Target: $1,000,000 ARR ≈ **$83,333/month in margin**. At $250/month average margin per customer, that's **333 paying customers**.

Using the 2.25% audit‑to‑customer conversion rate:

- 333 customers / 0.0225 = **14,800 audits needed**

Over 18 months: **~822 audits/month** (sustainable with free channels + a small paid budget).

What must be true:

- The free tool must consistently attract 800+ audits/month (via community, referrals, Credex's existing channels).
- The audit engine must surface >$500/month savings for at least 30‑40% of audits to feed the consultation pipeline.
- Credex must have enough discounted credit inventory to fulfil the demand.
- The consultation‑to‑purchase process must be streamlined (e.g., automated scheduling, pre‑calculated savings reports).

If conversion rates are even slightly better (e.g., 3% audit‑to‑customer) or average spend is higher (e.g., $5,000/month), the audit volume required drops significantly. The tool could drive $1M ARR with fewer than 10,000 total audits.
